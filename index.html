Welcome to Pune

Pune is a beautiful city in Maharashtra, India. It is known as the "Oxford of the East" due to its educational institutions.

It has a pleasant climate, rich culture, and a growing IT industry.

Pune Famous For:-

Pune is known as the "Oxford of the East" for its numerous educational institutions, is the cultural capital of Maharashtra, and is home to a thriving IT sector. The city boasts a rich historical past, serving as the former capital of the Maratha Empire and the seat of the Peshwas. Key landmarks include Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and Sinhagad Fort, reflecting its heritage. Pune also offers popular street foods, unique local snacks like Shrewsbury biscuits and chiwda, and is a major hub for the creation of Paithani sarees and Kolhapuri chappals.
